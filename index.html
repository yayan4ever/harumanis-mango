About Harumanis

The Harumanis mango, a treasure of Malaysia, is a premium mango variety grown exclusively in the northern state of Perlis. Its name, derived from the Malay words "harum" (fragrant) and "manis" (sweet), perfectly describes its captivating aroma and luscious flavor.

Known for its vibrant yellow-orange flesh and unparalleled sweetness, the Harumanis mango is a seasonal delicacy, available only from **April to June** each year. Its creamy texture and unique flavor profile make it a favorite among locals and international mango enthusiasts.

Cultivated with care by dedicated farmers in Perlis, the Harumanis mango thrives in the region's unique climate, characterized by dry weather and nutrient-rich soil. This combination ensures the exceptional quality of each fruit harvested.
